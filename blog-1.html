Is there a pet food for fussy cats?

The Meowlicious team

Cats are notorious for their picky eating habits and as cat owners, we want to ensure our furry friends receive the best nutrition possible. Here, we offer tips on feeding a fussy cat and how MeowliciousHalal Cat Food can help your feline enjoy mealtimes.

Understanding fussy eating in cats

Fussy eating can stem from a variety of factors, from a lack of routine to too many treats, which can be seen as a reward for refusing their own food. Common signs of a fussy cat include turning away food, playing with food, or displaying disinterest in mealtime altogether.

If your cat is displaying concerning eating behaviours or has not eaten for more than 24 hours, contact your vet immediately.

Meowlicious Halal Cat Food

Our halal-certified cat food is crafted with all natural ingredients and is free from artificial colours, additives and preservatives, and includes all the essential nutrients your cat needs to promote a healthy immune system.

Our protein-packed flavours have been tried and tested on the fussiest cats we know to ensure we have developed a cat food that your furry friend will love!

Tips to encourage fussy cats to eat

With patience and practice, you can encourage the pickiest of eaters to enjoy their meals. Consider your cat’s environment when they eat and ensure that mealtime is a calm and quiet experience away from any distractions, if you have any other concerns, always consult your vet for advice.
