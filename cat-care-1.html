How to look after your cat

The Meowlicious team

Bringing you tips and advice on looking after your cat

Welcoming a cat into the family is a delightful adventure filled with plenty of purrs, cuddles, and responsibilities. Here are our tips on caring for your cat, ensuring their safety, health, and happiness every step of the way.

Create a safe environment

Cats are territorial creatures by nature, so it’s important to provide, particularly indoor cats, with a safe and stimulating environment where they can run, climb, and stay active. Scratching posts and interactive toys such as mice can help your cat partake in hunting behaviours and keep them active and mentally stimulated.

It’s also important to designate a warm and dry area where your cat can snooze, which is away from any noise so they can rest and recharge.

Feed your cat a nutritious diet

A complete and balanced diet is fundamental to your cat’s health and well-being. When choosing a cat food, opt for food that is nutritious, high in protein and made from high-quality ingredients.

At Meowlicious, our three flavours of adult cat food, and our kitten food are full of natural ingredients from natural sources, are free from artificial additives and preservatives, and include all the essential nutrients that your cat needs, including Vitamin A, D, and E, to keep your furry friend happy and healthy.

Keep your cat healthy

Regular vet check-ups are essential to keep your cat healthy. Make sure to book regular health checks to address any health concerns, get advice, and stay up to date on vaccinations and flea prevention treatments. Keep an eye out for any changes in behaviour or signs of illness and contact your vet immediately if you have any concerns.

Love your cat

Above all, shower your cat with love, affection, and lots of cuddles, which will nurture your relationship and create a strong bond built on trust and mutual respect. By showing your cat love and compassion, you will forge a deep and lasting connection that enriches both of your lives.
