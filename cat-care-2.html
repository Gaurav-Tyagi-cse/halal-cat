CAT CARE

 

Helping your cat live a happy life

The Meowlicious team

Bringing you tips and advice on looking after your cat

A happy cat equals a happy owner, so it’s important to keep your furry friend happy and healthy. Here are our top tips to help your cat live a long, happy life through play, a delicious diet and lots of love.

Playing with your cat

Cats are curious creatures that need to be mentally and physically stimulated to avoid boredom. Toys such as wands, fish rods and soft mice, can keep your cat busy and help them engage in natural hunting behaviours.

Play with your cat daily to help them burn energy, stay happy, and avoid destructive behaviours, which include mistaking hands and feet for prey!

Feed your cat delicious food

A complete, balanced diet that is high in protein and filled with goodness is essential for your cat’s health and maintenance of their silky smooth coat.

Meowlicious Halal Cat Food comes in three delicious flavours, is 100% halal-certified, and is full of natural ingredients from natural sources. Our cat food includes all the essential nutrients your cat needs, such as Vitamin A, D, and E, to promote a healthy immune system, maintain a healthy heart, and keep bones strong.

Get to know your cat

As a cat owner, you will soon begin to recognise their body language, including important, subtle signs that they are happy, anxious or feeling unwell. If your furry friend approaches you with a curved upright tail this often means they are happy to see you!

Showing you their tummy, one of their most vulnerable areas, is your cat’s way of saying they trust you. Slow blinking is said to be a show of affection – the cat equivalent of a kiss!

If at any point you become concerned about your cat’s behaviour, please contact your vet immediately.

Love your cat

The most powerful way to help your cat live a happy life is through lots of love, snuggles, and quality bonding time. Giving your cat lots of attention every day, talking to them, and loving them will improve their overall health and wellbeing.
