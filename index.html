About our halal cat food

  • At Meowlicious, our team has been working hard to develop a dry cat food unique to the INDIA, tasking ourselves with creating delicious food for your furry friend that is 100% halal-certified, to bring into your home. We have tried and tested our flavors on some of the fussiest cats we know to ensure that we have developed a product that meets your pet’s unique needs!

The story so far

The story so far In 2023, Aihtsham Rashid faced the challenge of finding cat food in the India that met both Islamic standards of purity and the ethical treatment of animals. Determined to provide only the best for his beloved cats Ivy and Coco, Aihtsham decided to solve the problem himself and began searching globally for options that would guarantee the production of 100% halal cat food. From this journey, Meowlicious was born and is proud to offer halal-certified cat food that is healthy, nutritious, and full of natural ingredients.
