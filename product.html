Delicious cat food that’s 100% Halal

Your furry friend deserves only the best pet food that is healthy, nutritious, and full of flavour.
Meowlicious Halal Pet Foods is committed to providing beloved pets with the best care and nutrition, which is why our cat food is formulated with all-natural ingredients and is free from artificial additives and preservatives.
We’re proud to be the first halal pet food in the INDIA that is HMC halal-certified and free from cross-contamination, honouring Islamic guidelines and allowing you to extend your faith and ethical values into the life of your pet and your home.
