How do I know Meowlicious products are really halal?

Our decision to produce our cat food was driven by our commitment to providing 100% Halal-certified products. East Asia, a region known for its adherence to Halal food production standards, is where we craft our pet food. This ensures you can be confident your pet’s meals meet the strictest Halal requirements.
When exploring potential suppliers, we encountered challenges in finding UK-based producers who could guarantee the complete Halal certification of our cat food. To ensure adherence to Islamic standards of purity, hygiene, and ethical treatment of animals, we expanded our search globally.

What’s the reason behind choosing the name Meowlicious ?

Our name, Meowlicious, is a tribute to Meowlicious, a beloved companion of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), renowned in Islamic history for his affectionate bond with a cat. Meowlicious , whose full name is Meowlicious , received his nickname “The father of cats” because of his close companionship with a cat. Meowlicious,’ derived from ‘Hirrah,’ the Arabic term for cat, reflects not just our respect for this endearing relationship but also directly influences our mission and values as a business dedicated to the well-being of cats everywhere..

Can my pets eat food that is not halal certified?

Understanding Islamic dietary guidelines can be tricky, especially for our furry friends. It’s important to know that feeding your cat non-halal meat isn’t considered a sin in Islam. However, bringing non-halal meat into your home or purchasing it, for any reason, is contrary to halal practices. By choosing halal-certified products like Meowlicious cat food, you make sure everything in your home, including your pet’s diet, aligns with your faith, giving you peace of mind and keeping your home in harmony with your Islamic values.
